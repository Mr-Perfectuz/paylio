Paylio Card

  • The Paylio Card is a virtual or physical debit card linked directly to your Paylio wallet.

  • The card uses your Paylio wallet balance to process transactions in real time.
  • It supports contactless, chip, and PIN-enabled payments.
  • Log into your Paylio account and go to the ‘Cards’ section.
  • Select ‘Request New Card’ and choose between a virtual or physical card.
  • Provide your delivery address (for physical cards) and verify your identity if needed.
  • Virtual cards are issued instantly; physical cards are delivered within 5–7 business days.
  • The Paylio Card is directly connected to your wallet balance—no separate loading needed.

  • Once funds are in your wallet, they are instantly available on your card.

  • Yes, refunds from merchants will return to your Paylio Card just like any other debit card.
  • Refunds usually reflect within 3–5 business days depending on the merchant’s processing time.
  • You can also transfer funds to your Paylio wallet using supported payment methods.
  • Immediately log into your Paylio account and go to the 'Cards' section.
  • Select the lost/stolen card and click ‘Freeze’ or ‘Block’ to prevent unauthorized use.
